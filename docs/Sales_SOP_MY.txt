Sales SOP (Sample) — Malaysia Retail Chain
Version: 1.0

Daily Close:
1) Close sales at 10:00 PM.
2) Export sales transactions (CSV) for each branch.
3) Verify Total Sales by State/Branch/Product/Channel.
4) Submit KPI summary to management the next morning.

Management KPI Examples:
- “Sales bulan ni berapa?”
- “Banding sales bulan ni vs bulan lepas”
- “Top 3 product bulan 2024-06”
